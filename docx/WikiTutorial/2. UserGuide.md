# 📖 User Guide: How to Navigate CarDeals

Getting started with CarDeals is simple. Here is a full walkthrough of every feature available to you as a user.

---

## 1. 🏠 Home Page

When you first visit the site, you land on **`Home.jsx`** — the main landing page. It includes:
- A **hero banner** with a featured vehicle or promotion
- A **featured cars section** pulling live data from the inventory
- Call-to-action buttons linking to the Inventory and Financing pages

---

## 2. 🔍 Browsing the Inventory

Navigate to the **Inventory** page via the top navigation bar (`Header.jsx` → `NavMenu.jsx`).

- All car listings are fetched live from the database via `GET /api/cars`
- Listings are displayed in a **paginated grid** (`CarGrid.jsx`) of individual car cards (`CarCard.jsx`)
- Each card shows the car's **photo, make/model, year, mileage, price**, and a condition badge (`CarBadge.jsx`) — New, Used, Featured, or Sold

### Filtering & Searching
Use the **sidebar filter panel** (`Sidebar.jsx` + `FilterPanel.jsx`) to narrow results by:
- **Price range** — drag the min/max slider (`PriceRangeSlider.jsx`)
- **Category** — Sedan, SUV, Truck, Electric, Coupe, Van
- **Year, Mileage, Condition**

Use the **search bar** (`SearchBar.jsx`) in the header to search by make, model, year, or keyword.

---

## 3. 🚘 Viewing a Car's Detail Page

Click any car card to open its full detail page (`CarDetail.jsx`). Here you will find:

| Section | Component | What It Shows |
|---|---|---|
| Image Gallery | `CarImageGallery.jsx` | Scrollable, zoomable photos |
| Video Tour | `VideoPlayer.jsx` | HTML5 car walkthrough video |
| Specifications | `CarSpecs.jsx` | Engine, transmission, fuel type, mileage, color, etc. |
| Price History | `PriceHistoryChart.jsx` | Line chart of price changes over time |
| Condition Badge | `CarBadge.jsx` | New / Used / Featured / Sold label |
| Contact Button | `Button.jsx` | Links to the Contact page or opens a modal |

---

## 4. ⚖️ Comparing Vehicles

You can compare two or more vehicles side-by-side:

1. On any car card or detail page, click **"Add to Compare"**
2. Selected cars are stored in `CarContext.jsx` (global state)
3. Navigate to the **Compare page** (`Compare.jsx`) to see a full side-by-side spec table (`CarComparison.jsx`)

---

## 5. 💰 Financing Calculator

Go to the **Financing** page (`Financing.jsx`) to estimate your monthly payments:
- Enter the **vehicle price**, **down payment**, and **interest rate**
- The calculator computes your estimated monthly payment in real time
- Results are formatted using `formatCurrency.js` (e.g. `$24,500`)

---

## 6. 🗺️ Finding a Dealership

Visit the **Dealer Locator** page (`DealerLocator.jsx`):
- An interactive **Leaflet.js map** (`DealershipMap.jsx`) shows all branch locations
- Click any marker to see the dealership's name, address, phone, email, and hours
- Data is fetched live from `GET /api/dealers`

---

## 7. 🔐 Creating an Account & Logging In

### Register
- Go to **Register** (`Register.jsx`) and fill in your name, email, and password
- Your account is created via `POST /api/auth/register`
- Passwords are securely hashed before storage

### Login
- Go to **Login** (`Login.jsx`) and enter your email and password
- On success, a **JWT token** is returned and stored; it is automatically attached to all future requests via `api.js`
- Your login state is managed globally in `AuthContext.jsx`

### Forgot Password
- Use the **Forgot Password** page (`ForgotPassword.jsx`) to trigger a reset email

---

## 8. 👤 Your Private Account Area

Once logged in, you have access to four private pages (protected by `PrivateRoute.jsx`):

| Page | File | What You Can Do |
|---|---|---|
| Dashboard | `Dashboard.jsx` | Overview of your saved cars, recent activity, alerts |
| Profile | `Profile.jsx` | View and edit your name, email, and password |
| Saved Cars | `SavedCars.jsx` | Your bookmarked/wishlist vehicles |
| Notifications | `Notifications.jsx` | Alerts for price drops or new matching inventory |

---

## 9. 📱 Responsive Design

The site is fully responsive across all screen sizes:
- On **desktop** — full sidebar, expanded navigation, multi-column grid
- On **tablet/mobile** — the navigation collapses into a **hamburger menu** (`NavMenu.jsx`) and the layout adjusts to a single column

---

## 10. 🎨 Switching Themes

Use the **theme switcher** (`ThemeSwitcher.jsx`) in the navigation bar to change the site's visual style. Three themes are available:

| Theme | File | Look & Feel |
|---|---|---|
| Default (Light) | `theme-default.css` | Clean, professional, neutral tones |
| Dark | `theme-dark.css` | Sleek dark background with luxury accents |
| Sport | `theme-sport.css` | Bold red and black sporty scheme |

Your selected theme is managed by `ThemeContext.jsx` and applied globally across the app.

---

## 11. 🧭 First-Time Onboarding

If you are visiting for the first time, a step-by-step **interactive tour** (`TourGuide.jsx`) powered by React Joyride will highlight key features of the platform. You can also visit the **How-To Guide** page (`HowToGuide.jsx`) at any time for a written walkthrough of how to browse and buy a car.
